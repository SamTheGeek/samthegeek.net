<p>It seems that the return to a slightly irreverent tone in Apple's advertising (see <a href="http://samthegeek.net/blog/2014/4/gigantic-and-powerful">Gigantic and Powerful</a>) has continued. The latest ad, <a href="http://www.apple.com/macbook-air/stickers/#video-stickers">Stickers</a>, features the return of the rainbow Apple logo for the first time in over fifteen years. It flickers in. The ad isn't perfect, it's even a little bit messy. But that's what the company&nbsp;<em>feels&nbsp;</em>like. A bit messy. A bit nostalgic.</p><p>People seem to insist that this is a 'new Apple' - but it's really just the internal culture of Apple showing up a bit.</p>
